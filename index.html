About Us

It's that time of the year again! To sit with a good book and a cup of cinnamon tea in front of the fireplace. To hang stockings, make snowmen and spend time with your family. But it's also time for magic and miracles and being truly kind to one another. Christmas is all about giving. Not just giving out of tradition or responsibility. It's about giving to really make a difference and be a blessing to someone.

"Christmas angels" is a non-profit organization whose goal is to ensure Christmas gifts for as many children in foster care as possible. We believe that we can put smiles on the faces of these innocent and unfortunate souls and that you will help us to do so! Our close cooperation with toy distribution companies, foster care homes and hands on experience makes us experts in helping Santa Claus spread the happiness!
